Welcome to my little corner of the Tricity!

I created this space to share the places that make Gdańsk, Sopot, and Gdynia feel so special. These cities are full of stories — from the echo of old streets in Gdańsk, to the bright wooden piers of Sopot, to the modern seaside rhythm of Gdynia.

Here you’ll find a collection of my favorite spots: cozy cafés, quiet beaches, hidden viewpoints, and vibrant streets worth exploring. Every picture is a moment I loved, and every description is an invitation for you to discover it too.

Very soon, this website will grow into something more — a small trip-planning guide. You’ll be able to choose your favorite places on the map, see coordinates, and build your own route through the Tricity, step by step.

Let’s explore together. Let’s build your perfect trip. And if you ever feel lost — I’m here to guide you.
